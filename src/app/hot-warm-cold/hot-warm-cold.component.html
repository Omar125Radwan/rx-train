Hot Vs Cold Observables

Cold Observable

  1. Share one instance
  2. Should subscribe to return data
  3. Data coming from observable

Hot Observable

  1. Share more than one instance
    Share instance for all subscribers
    ex: subject
  2. Subject share same data to all subscribers
  3. Shouldn't subscribe to return data

Warm Observable

  1. Between the two
  2. From cold: should do subscribe
  3. From hot: Return multicast data

?* Some Notes

  1. publish() & connect() & ConnectableObservable
    transform the Cold to Warm
  2. refCount() do connect
  3. share() == publish() + refCount()
    Make Observable Multicast
